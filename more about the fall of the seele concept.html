    ਪਵਣੁ ਪਾਣੀ ਅਗਨੀ ਪਾਤਾਲ ॥

    ਕੇਤੇ ਪਵਣ ਪਾਣੀ ਵੈਸੰਤਰ ਕੇਤੇ ਕਾਨਿ ਮਹੇਸੁ ॥

    ਤਿਤੁ ਸਰਵਰੈ ਭਏਲੇ ਨਿਵਾਸਾ ਪਾਣੀ ਪਾਵਕ ਤਿਨਹਿ ਕੀਆ ॥

    paanee dhotai autaras kheh

    --water can wash away the dirt--

    gaavahi tuhano paun paanee baisantar gaavai raajaa dharam dhuaare

    --the praanic wind, water and fire sing; The righteous judge of Dharma sings to Your Door--

    pavan paanee aganee paataal

    --wind, water, fire and the nether regions--

    KETE PAVAN PAANEE VAISANTAR KETE KAAN MAHES

    --so many winds, waters and fires; so many Krishnas and Shivas--

    tit saravarai bhe’eele nivaasaa paanee paavak tinahi keeeaa

    --in that pool, people have made their homes, but the water there is as hot as fire!--
