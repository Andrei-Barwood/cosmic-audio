The Story

This EP has been in the works for over a year. I wanted to create something that captured the feeling of Berlin at 5 AM ‚Äì that liminal space between night and day, exhaustion and euphoria, darkness and light.

Every track was recorded in single takes using only hardware synths and drum machines. No computer, no editing, no safety net. What you'll hear is raw, unfiltered, and completely honest.
