Underground Sessions Vol. 5

A Journey Through Dark Techno

Duration 90:00
BPM Range 126-135
Genre Dark Techno
Recorded Sept 2025

Welcome to the fifth installment of Underground Sessions, my monthly deep dive into the darker, more experimental corners of electronic music. This 90-minute journey represents pure, unfiltered techno energy captured in one take.

The Vibe

Industrial soundscapes meet hypnotic grooves. Raw, uncompromising, and built for the late-night dancefloor.

Best For

Peak-time sets, warehouse parties, after-hours sessions, or late-night studio work.

Equipment Used

3x Pioneer CDJ-3000, DJM-900NXS2 Mixer, Allen & Heath Xone:96 for effects.

Recording Location

Home Studio, Berlin • Recorded at 3 AM with all the lights off for maximum immersion.

The Story Behind The Mix

For this session, I wanted to explore the tension between industrial soundscapes and hypnotic grooves. The result is a sonic narrative that builds from brooding atmospherics into relentless, driving percussion, before descending back into the void.

I've been collecting these tracks over the past few months, hunting through Bandcamp's underground corners, digging through promo pools, and rediscovering forgotten gems from my collection. Every track here tells a story, and together they create something greater than the sum of their parts.

"The beauty of underground music is in its rawness. There's no compromise, no commercial pressure – just pure artistic expression."

The opening section features unreleased material from some of my favorite emerging producers. Around the 30-minute mark, things start to intensify as I introduce harder, more industrial elements. The final third strips everything back down to abstract, deconstructed rhythms.
